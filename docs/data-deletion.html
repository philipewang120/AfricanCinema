User Data Deletion

Last Updated: June 2026

African Cinema allows users to request deletion of their personal data.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to philipewang120@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address associated with your account

Data Removed

  • Account information
  • Profile information
  • Ratings and reviews associated with your account
  • Watchlists and user-generated content where applicable

Facebook Users

You may also remove African Cinema from your Facebook account's Apps and Websites settings.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days.

Contact

Email: philipewang120@gmail.com
